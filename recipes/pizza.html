Pizza

Description

Pizza, a beloved culinary delight, needs no introduction. Its allure lies in the perfect marriage of a crisp, yet doughy crust, topped with a symphony of flavors. From the classic Margherita adorned with vibrant tomato, fresh mozzarella, and basil, to the adventurous combinations of toppings that span the globe, pizza caters to every palate. It's a communal pleasure, shared among friends and family, a comfort food that transcends borders and brings people together. Whether enjoyed in the heart of Naples, where it was born, or savored in a cozy pizzeria on a Friday night, pizza's universal appeal is undeniable. Its versatility and ability to cater to individual tastes make it a timeless favorite, destined to delight taste buds for generations to come.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • Pizza Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Toppings
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Cornmeal or Flour

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to the highest temperature (475-500°F or 245-260°C) with a pizza stone or baking sheet inside.
  2. Roll out pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness and place it on parchment paper.
  3. Spread a thin layer of sauce on the dough, leaving a border for the crust.
  4. Add a generous layer of cheese, then your desired toppings.
  5. Drizzle olive oil, season with salt and pepper.
  6. Transfer the pizza on parchment paper to the preheated oven.
  7. Bake for 10-15 minutes until the crust is golden and cheese is bubbly.
  8. Remove and let cool for a minute, then slice and serve hot.
